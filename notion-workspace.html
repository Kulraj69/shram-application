Executive Summary
💡
What is Shram?
An AI-powered desktop agent that finds follow-ups and executes them automatically. Monitors WhatsApp, Gmail, and Calendar to detect when responses are needed, drafts context-aware replies using on-device memory, and executes with one-click.
